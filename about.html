About

The Conservation Compendium is a resource for finding out more about various conservation conditions affecting different materials. This will help you identify the issues your collection is facing and when consulting a conservator about what treatment might be required. This is still a work-in-progress site which will be updated with more information. Reach out if you'd like to be part of this project!

The information found here provided should never replace consultation with a trained conservator, but to empower you to begin your journey in the art and science of caring for your collections.
