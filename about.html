About

The Conservation Compendium is a resource for understanding various conservation conditions affecting different materials. This will help you identify the issues your collection is facing and help you when consulting a conservator about what treatment might be required. The details provided should not replace consultation with a trained conservator, but to empower you to begin your journey in the art and science of caring for your collections.
